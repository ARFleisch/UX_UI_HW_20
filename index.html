BBR Travel Application

Solo Project

The project goal was to research and create a mobile travel app that completed two tasks.
Task one: New user sign up process. Task two: Book travel plans.

Research

Interview Plan

Recruit five 35-45 year old, tech-savvy, working professionals who enjoy travel.
Conduct a ten-question user interview one-on-one either by in-person or video call.

Problem Statement

Anne who is a tech-savvy, working professional needs a tool that combines the many
aspects of travel planning (budgeting, location, etc.) into one place because using multiple
resources across different platforms becomes overwhelming and time-consuming.
